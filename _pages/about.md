I am a research assistant in the [Computer Vision & Machine Learning Lab](https://homepage.iis.sinica.edu.tw/~liutyng/) at the [Institute of Information Science (IIS)](https://www.iis.sinica.edu.tw/en/index.html), Academia Sinica, under the mentorship of Prof. [Tyng-Luh Liu](https://homepage.iis.sinica.edu.tw/pages/liutyng/index_en.html). My research lies at the nexus of computer vision and energy-efficient systems. Specifically, I am focused on the development of tiny-machine learning models and unsupervised (self-supervised) representation learning within the field of computer vision. 

Prior to joining IIS, I completed the Master's degree in [Integrated Circuits & Systems group (ICS)](https://giee.ntu.edu.tw/en/ics_lecture.php) from the [Graduate Institute of Electronics Engineering](https://giee.ntu.edu.tw/en/) at [National Taiwan University (NTU)](https://www.ntu.edu.tw/english/). This followed my undergraduate studies at [National Chiao Tung University (NCTU)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Chiao_Tung_University).
